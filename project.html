Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp sunflower oil
  • 2 small onions
  • 4 garlic cloves
  • All purpose seasoning(amount to taste)
  • Mixed herbs (To taste)
  • 300g deboned chicken thigh
  • Pinch of sugar
  • 400g chopped tomatoes
  • 400g black beans
  • 500g cooked rice
  • 8 flour or corn tortillas

Easy Chicken Burritos

Prep Time: 25 minutes

Cook Time: 30 minutes

All Ingredients used are linked in the Ingredients section which will take you directly to the Tesco store front to the correct item

Method

Step 1

Cut the chicken thighs into cubes approximately 2cm in length. Season the chicken in a bowl using the all purpose and mixed herbs seasonings and let sit for at least 30 mins

Step 2

Finely chop the onions and set aside. Heat the oil in a large pan or pot to medium high heat. Add the onions, garlic and any additional seasoning desired.

Step 3

Add the marinated chicken to the pan and allow to pan fry for 5 minutes then add sugar to the pan and cook for additional 5 minutes.

Step 4

Add chopped tomatoes to the pan and simmer for 5 minutes

Step 5

Add beans to the pan and water from the can. Season, stir and simmer everything together for 20 minutes

Step 6

To make into burritos, heat the tortillas to the instructions on the packet. Pile some rice and chicken sauce along each tortilla and scatter over your choice of toppings. Fold over the ends and roll up to seal. Secure by wrapping with foil for storage if needed.

Step 7

Enjoy!!!!
